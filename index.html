Keeping Fit In Lockdown

Posted on 10/07/2020

So the world has shutdown! Scary right? Everything seems weird, what day is it even? Is
there any purpose? Should I keep working out? What's the purpose of it all?
To answer these questions we have to start from the top. What was the motivating factor for you?
Was it the clothes you'll wear? or the way people will accept you?
In order to keep up with good habits, we have to find the motivation from internal factors not external factors.
Everythin g changes except you. At the end of the day, you're all you've got, you're the one that's left to deal with the you, you've created.
Do you want that you to be a self-loathing and validation seeking you? or do you want that you to be a healthy satisfied you?
